Jake's Steak

This steak is very delicious. Let's make it!

Required Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • Garlic
  • Oil for cooking
  • Shallots or Onions

Steps for Cooking

Prep

  1. Brine steak
  2. Thaw steak
  3. Chop onions, garlic

Pickup

  1. Put steak on pan, very hot
  2. Flip at 3 minutes
  3. Cook until 135 F
  4. Add butter and garlic, baste
  5. Enjoy
