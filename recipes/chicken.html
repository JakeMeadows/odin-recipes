Jake's Chicken

This chicken is very amazing. Let's make it!

Required Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Lots of seasoning
  • End of list

Steps for Cooking

Prep

  1. Dry off chicken
  2. Season chicken
  3. Let it season and come to room temp

Pickup

  1. Add to air fryer
  2. Put on for 190 F
  3. Flip at 4 min
  4. Cook until internal temp of 74 C
