Jake's Eggs

These eggs are so good. Let's make them!

Required Ingredients

  • Large chicken eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Onions

Steps for Cooking

Prep

  1. Chop onions and garlic
  2. Get ready egg container

Pickup

  1. Get pan hot, add olive oil
  2. Add onions and garlic
  3. Add eggs
  4. Cook eggs until desired quality
  5. Enjoy
